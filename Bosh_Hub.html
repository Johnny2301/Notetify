About

While listening during class, it is inevitable to lose the appetite of copying notes due to the fast discussion of the professor. Slam that notebook and pen and you'll end up relying to your photographic memory and "blueban" method. Your second option would be taking a snap and sending it to your class' chat room if you have a good heart and don't want to graduate alone. However, at your chat room, all of your snaps are mixing up with other subjects making it difficult for you to locate those notes. That's a hassle! Worry not future engineer, presenting to you is a platform where you can access all of the notes of each of your subject.

NOTETIFY allows you to post and easily download the necessary notes that can help you in studying and coping up with your grades. We display the list of the subjects, in an organized manner, where you can find the notes you want to study with few clicks away. All you have to do is to listen well to your professor and understand thoroughly the topics and on the other end, NOTETIFY have your notes ready.
